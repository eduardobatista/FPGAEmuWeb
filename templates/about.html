Credits

The DE2 Emulator has been developed by Prof. Eduardo L. O. Batista (web front and back ends) and Prof. Fabian L. Cabrera (emulation backend), both from the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC).

Platforms and Dependencies

Emulation backend: GHDL-based.

Web backend: Python and Flask.

Web frontend: MDBootstrap, JQuery, SocketIO, and the outstanding Ace Editor.
