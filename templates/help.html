Authorship

The FPGA Emulator Web has been developed by Prof. Eduardo L. O. Batista. The emulation backend is based on the work of Prof. Fabian L. Cabrera at the FPGAEmu project. Both are professors at the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC).

Platforms and Dependencies

Emulation backend: GHDL-based.

Web backend: Python and Flask.

Web frontend: Bulma, JQuery, SocketIO, SVG.js, JQuery.Ui, and the outstanding Ace Editor.

General Instructions

The following ports are mapped to the FPGA Emulator Web:

FPGAEmuWeb Port List
CLK_500Hz: in std_logic;
CLK_1Hz: in std_logic;
CLK_10Hz: in std_logic;
KEY: in std_logic_vector(3 downto 0);
SW: in std_logic_vector(17 downto 0);
LEDR: out std_logic_vector(17 downto 0);
HEX0,HEX1,HEX2,HEX3,HEX4,HEX5,HEX6,HEX7 : out std_logic_vector(6 downto 0);
