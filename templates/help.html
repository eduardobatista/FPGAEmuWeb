Authors

The FPGA Emulator Web has been developed by Prof. Eduardo L. O. Batista (web front and back ends) and Prof. Fabian L. Cabrera (emulation backend), both from the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC).

Platforms and Dependencies

Emulation backend: GHDL-based.

Web backend: Python and Flask.

Web frontend: MDBootstrap, JQuery, SocketIO, and the outstanding Ace Editor.

General Instructions

The following ports are mapped to the FPGA Emulator Web:

  • CLK_500Hz: in std_logic;
  • CLK_1Hz: in std_logic;
  • CLK_10Hz: in std_logic;
  • KEY: in std_logic_vector(3 downto 0)
  • SW: in std_logic_vector(17 downto 0)
  • LEDR: out std_logic_vector(17 downto 0)
  • HEX0,HEX1,HEX2,HEX3,HEX4,HEX5,HEX6,HEX7 : out std_logic_vector(6 downto 0)
